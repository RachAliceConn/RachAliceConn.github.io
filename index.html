What kind of UK companies spend money lobbying the EU?

How much do they spend?

And what on?


### Holds plot
There are currently 381 UK Headquartered companies on the EU Transparency Register.

The register records donations to lobbying interest groups.

Companies donate to a range of causes, but some are far more popular than others. 164 companies donated to Research and Technology, for example. While only32 donated to promote youth.


## Holds plot
Some of the UK companies which spend the most on lobbying in Brussels also earn the most in grants.

Here, and in the following graph you can explore the way money travels between UK companies and the EU.


Samsung, GlaxoSmithKline, BT and Rolls Royce are all in the top 20 companies for donations to, and grants from, the EU.


FTSE 100 companies, the biggest in the UK by market capitalisation, also donate vast sums to lobbying initiatives in the EU.
